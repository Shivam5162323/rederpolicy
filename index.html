Privacy Policy for Reder

Last Updated: 9/3/25

1. Introduction

Welcome to Reder, a PDF manager app that allows you to store, view, and manage your documents—including PDFs, Excel, and CSV files—from any Android device. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register for an account, we collect and store your username for identification purposes and to personalize your experience.

Files and Documents

Our App enables you to store your PDFs. These files are securely stored on our servers so that you can access them anywhere.

Organizational Data

You can create folders to organize your files. The information regarding your folder structure is stored to enhance your file management experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the functionalities of the App:

  • Storing and displaying your documents.
  • Enabling you to organize your files into folders.
  • Allowing you to download documents for offline access.

We do not analyze your files or use them for any purpose other than to operate the App.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your username and files are stored on secure servers with industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access. Files downloaded for offline viewing are stored locally on your device, and you are advised to secure your device accordingly.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or trade your personal information or files with any third parties. Disclosure of your data may only occur if required by law, and only the minimal necessary information will be shared.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access and correct your account information. You may also request deletion of your account and associated data. Please note that once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

7. Third-Party Services

Our App may contain links or integrations with third-party services that have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies for further information on how they handle your data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will become effective immediately upon posting. We recommend that you review this policy periodically.
